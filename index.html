About Me

I graduated from UT Austin with a degree in Architecture. After several years, I returned to UT Austin to study Computer Science. While in school, I landed a great job as a game developer and pursued software development as a new career. I took time off to focus on family and became a full-time stay-at-home mom. When I returned to work, I transitioned into technical software sales. Having a good grasp for communication with both developers and end-users has been a valuable skillset in my current role as a member of the software team for an engineering firm in the oil and gas industry.


I have always loved problem-solving and design. I also possess constant desire to learn. I am once again return to UT Austin to study full stack web development. Becoming a full-stack web developer will be an exciting challenge and a perfect way to combine my love of design and coding.
